Learning Management System (LMS)

The challenge was building a single platform where admins, teachers, and students could each have a completely different experience — without the complexity bleeding into each other. The result was a full-featured LMS with a clean RBAC architecture that made managing courses, assessments, and progress feel effortless for every role.

Integrated external grading system API to auto-sync results into the LMS database
